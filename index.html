About Us

Established in the year 2021 by Jazim Latif, Joeeta Bagchi, Hajera Rahman, Yonis Abdulahi and Daniel Quagraine, F.E. Sports has long been established as the leading UK specialist retailer of fashionable branded, sports and casual wear.

What started as a single shop in East London has now expanded across the UK, and by late 2021, F.E. had opened a total of 56 stores. By 2022, F.E. will dominate most of the EU, primarily in Germany, Ireland, France, Netherlands and Spain, with Denmark and Sweden to follow suit. At this moment in time, F.E. is already regarded as the most innovative visual merchandiser of sportswear with the best and most exclusive stylish range.

Fast forward to early 2023, F.E. will officially launch its first store in Asia, in Malaysia. In November, F.E. will open its first flagship store in Pavilion Elite, Kuala Lumpur, and its third store in Asia in Sunway Velocity, Cheras.

F.E. looks forward to a progressive development in Asia, and the world in the days to come.
